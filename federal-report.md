# Federal Procurement Contracts Executed in 2025

## Dataset Summary

**Contracts Executed:** 64,930  
**Total Contract Value:** $97,692,769,058  
**Unique Suppliers:** 21,789  
**Data Source:** AusTender OCDS API  
**Period:** January 1 - December 31, 2025  

**Important:** Contract values represent maximum amounts over the full contract life, which may span multiple years. This dataset includes procurement contracts only and excludes social security payments, Medicare, grants, state transfers, and public service salaries.

**Supplier Counting Methodology:** Suppliers are counted by exact legal entity name as recorded in AusTender. Corporate groups with multiple legal entities (e.g., Thales Australia, Thales Australia Limited) are counted separately as distinct ABN holders.

## Methodology

**Data Collection:** AusTender OCDS API, all federal contract awards with execution dates in 2025  
**Analysis Period:** January 1, 2025 - December 31, 2025  
**Data Fields:** Contract ID, supplier name/ABN, procuring entity, contract value, dates, procurement method, supplier location, UNSPSC codes  
**Analysis Script:** [`analyze_federal_contracts.py`](https://github.com/taxpayer-money/australian-government-contracts/blob/main/analyze_federal_contracts.py)  
**Raw Data:** [`data/austender_2025_contracts.csv`](https://github.com/taxpayer-money/australian-government-contracts/blob/main/data/austender_2025_contracts.csv)

**Limitations:** 
- Supplier names reflect legal entity registration, not ultimate corporate ownership
- Subcontracting relationships not visible in public data
- Classified Defence contract details excluded
- Contract performance and outcomes not included
